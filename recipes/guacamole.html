Description

Guacamole is the ultimate blend of creamy, zesty, and fresh — a simple dish that captures the heart of Mexican flavor in every bite. Ripe avacodos are mashed to the perfect texture, then mixed with diced tomatoes, red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice for brightness. Each ingredient adds its own layer of flavor — the richness of the avocado, the crunch of the onion, the heat of the chili, and the citrusy tang that ties it all together.

Served as a dip with warm tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, burritos, and grilled meats, guacamole brings balance and freshness to any meal. It's quick to make, endlessly customizable, and always a crowd-please. Whether you're preparing a full Mexican spread or just need a snack that feels like sunshine in abowl, this guacamole recipe delivers creamy perfection and bold, satisfying flavor every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted.
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped.
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced. Apple cider vinegar is good too.
  • 1 lime, juiced.
  • salt and pepper to taste.

Instructions

  1. Prep the ingredients: finely chop the onion, chop and lightly seed the tomato to reduce excess liquid, mince hte garlic, and juice the lime.
  2. In a medium bowl, add the peeled, pitted avocados and mash with a fork until mostly smooh but still a little chunky.
  3. Sprinkle in the onion, tomato, and garlic. Add about half of the lime juice to start and, if you like extra tang, add a small splash (1/4-1 teaspoon) of apple cider vinegar.
  4. Season with a generous pinch of salt and a few rinds of black pepper. Gently fold everything together so the avocado stays a bit chunky.
  5. Taste and adjust: add more lime juice, salt, and pepper as needed. If using, add another tiny splash of vinegar until the flavor pops.
  6. Let the guacamole rest for 5-10 minutes o meld flavors, then taste oncemore and adjust seasoning.
  7. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, burritos, or grilled meats.
  8. To store briefly, press plastic wrap directly onto the surface and refrigerate up to 1 day; stir and brighten with a little fresh lime juice and salt before serving.
